## üìù RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Source Quality Criteria
1. **Primary sources preferred:** O*NET, official job descriptions, platform templates
2. **Authoritative secondary:** McKinsey, BCG, HBR, Gartner
3. **Practitioner-validated:** Notion/Asana templates (used by thousands)
4. **Recent:** 2024-2026 sources prioritized
5. **Cited:** All claims traceable to source

### Validation Process
1. Cross-reference across 3+ sources
2. Compare to Aditya's real-world experience
3. Test with practitioners in each role
4. Update quarterly as practices evolve
